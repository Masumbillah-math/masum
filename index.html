What is Environmental Solution?

Now more than ever, bussiness and governments recognize the need to incorporate environmenta concerns into their decision making. Arcadis is a global leader in inventive technical and financial approaches, helping some of the worlds leading corporates and governments understand their impact on the natural world.

Any kind of environmental issues or problems detemination, planing and solve respectivly is called Environmental Solution.

Role of EST Department, JUST for Environmental Solution:

Environmental Science & Technology-EST department was established in 2009 at Jessore University of Science & Technology-JUST, Jessore in Bangladesh.

BSc, MS and PhD courses are going on there. Students are contributing there using merit for environmental solution in bangladesh and also internationally. Their research on environment already published in many national and international journals.
